Crafted by masters, curated for you.

Rosanna Casano

Rosanna Casano, born in Marsala (Sicily) in 1968, been resident in Barcelona since 1989. Self-taught, she soon entered the artistic circles of the city.

Marcelo Fuentes

Marcelo Fuentes chooses his spectators among those capable of establishing sensorial, intellectual and emotional complicities with his creations.

Jordi Martoranno

Graduated in the Faculty of Fine Arts at the University of Barcelona, he has made several artistic residencies in Paris, Toulouse, Ustka and New York. He won the painting prize of the Vila Casas Foundation in Barcelona.

Carla Tarruela

Her works are characterized by an absence of symbolism and a search for suggestion through the chromatic range, shapes, textures and shades to create a visual poetry that suggests individuality and reflection.

Aurelio San Pedro

He studied electrical engineering and finished his studies in geomantics. After working for years as head of topography in Fcc he decided to study a Higher Diploma in Mural Arts in La Massana.

Anke Blaue

Anke Blaue work emerges from the material, it is the material that communicates to us what we are looking for, proposing a direct relationship, without obstacles, without even the slightest hint of her signature.
